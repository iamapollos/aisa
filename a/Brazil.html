Introduction

Our association members are part of the Ashinaga Africa Initiative , which consists of scholars and graduates dedicated to bringing a positive change to Africa. AISA members are studying various majors in different universities across the globe. Our population today (April 2020), approximates 400 with roughly 8 scholars from each of the 49 sub-Saharan countries in Africa.
