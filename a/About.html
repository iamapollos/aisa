About US

History

AISA was founded in 2016 by some African scholars in AAI. Its main objectives at that time included to get AAI scholars active in Bokin (fundraising), and to promote cultural exchange between Africa and Japan. AISA has since grown in size and scope of activities. From 2017 to 2020, AISA took up additional roles as an Information centre for sharing opportunities, promoting professional skills development. Having brought the AISA family together, on one platform, we are now pushing towards joint collaborative efforts to implement some of the projects we have been working to improve our motherland.

What we Do

To aid individual and collective growth of members through facilitating unity, networking and co-operation within AAI in a development context i.e Sharing information & opportunities, hosting professional development events.
